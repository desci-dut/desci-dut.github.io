Special Session on

 

Data-Driven Decision Making with Uncertainty

 

http://www.iske2019.lnnu.edu.cn

 

The 2019 IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Systems and Knowledge Engineering (ISKE 2019) is the 14th in a series of ISKE conferences. The conference will be held in Dalian, a beautiful place in northeast of China.

 

The conference proceedings will be published by IEEE Press (EI indexed). Special issues of SCI indexed journals will be devoted to a strict refereed selection of extended papers presented at ISKE 2019.

 

Scope and Motivation

 

The rapid development of ICT has made it much easier to collect and analyze data from different sources. Nowadays many decision making problems can be solved with the use of data analytics, i.e., data-driven decision making. However, in the process of data-driven decision making, different types of uncertainties in representation, processing and analysis can be identified due to the inherent characteristics of big data, for instance, the fuzziness, impreciseness and noise in the data, which brings many challenges for data-driven decision making. Thanks to the techniques of machine learning and computational intelligence, these uncertainties can be better handled to make high quality decisions. Therefore, the aim of this special session is to provide a forum for researchers and practitioners working in related areas to discuss the state-of-the-art in data-driven decision making with uncertainty by sharing their new ideas and results, and to foster possible collaborations.

 

The topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

 

- Uncertainty representation and processing techniques in data-driven decision making

- Big data analytics and machine learning in data-driven decision making

- Managing uncertainties in data-driven decision support systems

- Linguistic preference modelling in data-driven decision making

- Consistency and consensus modelling in data-driven decision making

- Decision making under social networks and opinion dynamics

- Uncertainty management in recommender systems

- Application of data-driven decision making methods, such as healthcare, supply chain management, marketing.
